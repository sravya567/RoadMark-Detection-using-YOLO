Road Mark Detection

Our project is dedicated to detect road marks in images.

  • The ROAD MARK Dataset is tailored for road safety applications, concentrating on detecting road markings, lane lines, arrows, pedestrian crossings, speed limit signs, and other relevant objects like traffic lights and barriers.
  • With 2892 samples, the dataset spans various road scenarios, including highways, urban streets, and rural areas. This diversity ensures models can handle real-world challenges such as different lighting conditions and road types.
  • The dataset is divided into three parts (Train, Valid, Test), enabling effective model training, validation, and evaluation. This systematic approach ensures robust performance in detecting and classifying road-related objects.
